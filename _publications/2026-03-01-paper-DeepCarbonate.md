This paper presents DeepCarbonate, a cleaned and standardized benchmark dataset for carbonate thin-section image analysis. The dataset contains 22 lithological categories organized by optical mode and split into train, validation, and test subsets in ImageNet format. The study evaluates multiple deep learning models, including ResNet, VGG, DenseNet, MobileNet, and EfficientNet, providing a reproducible benchmark for carbonate petrography research.

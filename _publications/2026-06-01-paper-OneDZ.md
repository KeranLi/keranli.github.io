This paper presents OneDZ, a global comprehensive detrital zircon U-Pb geochronology and Lu-Hf isotope database. The database integrates sample sources, locations, stratigraphy, depositional ages, elemental information, and isotopic data, and discusses the construction of large geoscience databases using artificial intelligence and programming workflows.
